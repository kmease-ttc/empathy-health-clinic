## Implementation Best Practices

Begin with a pilot program, perhaps focusing on one provider or specific appointment types. This allows for workflow refinement before full deployment.

### Gather Feedback
Regular team meetings to discuss AI scribe performance can identify areas for improvement and share best practices.
