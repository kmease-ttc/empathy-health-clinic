## How to Become a Mental Health Counselor

Becoming a mental health counselor requires dedication and a structured educational path. Start with a bachelor's degree in psychology or a related field. Follow this with a master's degree in counseling or mental health. After completing your education, obtaining a license is crucial. In Florida, aspiring counselors must pass the National Counselor Examination (NCE) and complete supervised clinical hours.
