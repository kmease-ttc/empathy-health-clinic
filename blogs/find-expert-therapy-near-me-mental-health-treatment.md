### Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) is a widely used treatment that focuses on identifying and changing negative thought patterns and behaviors. It’s particularly effective for conditions like depression and anxiety. In Winter Park, Orlando, many therapists offer CBT to help patients develop healthier coping mechanisms. For example, if you’re struggling with social anxiety, a CBT therapist might work with you to challenge irrational fears and gradually expose you to social situations in a controlled manner.

### Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)
Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT) is a specialized form of CBT designed to help individuals with intense emotions and borderline personality disorder. DBT combines individual therapy with group skills training sessions. It focuses on four key areas: mindfulness, distress tolerance, emotion regulation, and interpersonal effectiveness. If you’re in Winter Park, Orlando, and find yourself overwhelmed by emotions, DBT can provide practical skills to manage these feelings effectively.

### Medication Management
For some individuals, medication can be a crucial component of mental health treatment. Psychiatrists in Winter Park, Orlando, can work with you to find the right medication and dosage to manage symptoms effectively. Whether you’re dealing with depression, anxiety, or bipolar disorder, medication management can be an essential part of your treatment plan. It’s important to have regular consultations to monitor progress and make necessary adjustments.
