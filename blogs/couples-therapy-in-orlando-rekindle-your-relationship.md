## Types of Couples Therapy Available

In Orlando, couples therapy offers a path to rekindle your relationship through various approaches tailored to your unique needs. Understanding the different types of therapy can help you choose the best fit for you and your partner. Here are some popular options:

### Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT)

Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT) is designed to help couples understand and transform their emotional responses. This approach focuses on building a secure bond between partners by addressing attachment issues. In Orlando, many couples find EFT effective in reducing conflict and increasing intimacy. Research shows that approximately 70-75% of couples move from distress to recovery using EFT. Consider this option if you and your partner struggle with emotional disconnects.

### Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) for Couples

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) for Couples helps partners identify and change negative thought patterns that affect their relationship. This method is practical and goal-oriented, making it ideal for couples who want to develop healthier communication skills. In Orlando, therapists often recommend CBT for couples dealing with specific issues like trust or communication breakdowns. By focusing on actionable strategies, CBT can help you and your partner create lasting change.

### Gottman Method Couples Therapy

The Gottman Method is based on over 40 years of research and focuses on building a strong foundation of friendship, managing conflict, and creating shared meaning. This method is particularly beneficial for couples who want to enhance their relationship's overall quality. In Orlando, therapists trained in the Gottman Method offer [compassionate care options](https://empathyhealthclinic.com/in-person-meaning-therapy-guide/) that emphasize understanding and respect. Consider this approach if you're looking to deepen your connection and improve relationship satisfaction.
