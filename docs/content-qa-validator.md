# Content QA / Policy Validator

## Purpose
Enforce quality, SEO, and medical compliance standards.

## Checks Performed
- Structure and heading hierarchy
- Thin or low-value content
- Over-optimization
- Missing disclaimers
- Missing FAQs or schema
- E-E-A-T signals
- Medical compliance rules

## Output
- Quality score
- Violation list
- Required fixes

## Gating Rule
Content cannot be published without passing QA.
